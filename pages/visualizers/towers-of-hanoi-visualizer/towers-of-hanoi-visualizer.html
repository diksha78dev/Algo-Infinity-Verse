Algorithm Info

Towers of Hanoi is a mathematical puzzle where the objective is to move a stack of disks from one peg to another following these rules:

1. Move only one disk at a time.
2. Only the top disk of a stack can be moved.
3. No disk may be placed on top of a smaller disk.
